This will be a simple site logging my woodworking experiences.

I will try to update it as my projects move forward, and I intend to slowly add posts for older projects.

A few technical details:

  • It is written in Markdown.
  • Hugo is responsible for turning the content into a website. I considered others as well, but Hugo seems to have the best support.
  • The site is also hosted on IPFS as an alternative.
  • The theme is based on hugo-theme-texify, I enjoy the academic look of LaTeX.
